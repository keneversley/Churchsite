THE CHURCH

OUR CHURCH BELIEVES
IN JESUS AND LOVE 

The son of Arzo & Clara Miller, Rev. Josh Miller was born and raised in Greensburg, KY. A graduate of Green County High School and Campbellsville University (Bachelor of Science— Health & Physical Education, MAT/Health and P.E., MTH degree in Pastoral Leadership & Counseling all from Campbellsville University. 

Rev. Miller has served as the Youth Pastor at Heritage International Christian Church, the Lifetime Enrichment Leader at the Carl M. Radcliff Veteran Center, Behavior Reform Coach at Green County Middle School while also serving as the Freshman Boys Basketball Head Coach and Assistant Varsity Coach at Green County High School during his time in the Green County education system. 

Rev. Miller is currently an Exceptional Education Specialist and Assistant Boys Basketball Coach at John Hardin High School in Elizabethtown, KY where he now resides with his wife Mrs. Courtney Miller & daughter Kinleigh Ariah Miller. 

Rev. Miller is a born again, spirit filled child of God who is in constant pursuit of the will of God and advancing His kingdom. Rev. Miller loves God’s Word and God’s people bag has a passion for helping them enter into a relationship with Jesus Christ through service and sharing of the gospel
